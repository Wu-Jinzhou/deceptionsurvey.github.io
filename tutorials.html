4Risk Assessment Framework

Understand how to evaluate and categorize deception risks using our 5-level taxonomy.

Risk Levels:

  • R1: Cognitive Misleading - Surface-level deceptions
  • R2: Strategic Manipulation - Behavioral deceptions
  • R3: Objective Misgeneralization - Goal-level deceptions
  • R4: Institutional Erosion - System-level impacts
  • R5: Capability Concealment - Existential risks
